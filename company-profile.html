Company Profile

Driven By A Mission

Our goal is to provide the customer with an enjoyable, honest service by satisfying individual customers with quality products. We are committed to providing fair wages, reasonable, structured work schedules, clear duties, and spheres of rights and responsibilties for each team member.

Focused Vision

We are committed towards bringing inspiration and innovation to everything we do and create. More so, to emerge as the fastest growing, most rewarding and most transformative company.

Product Portfolio

Jyotika Appliances takes pride in manufacturing channels for LPG Glass Stoves and Gas Geyser Kits. Apart from that we are also involved in manufacturing of Glass & Stainless Steel Cooktops, cooktop parts, sheet metal parts. Add to that, we have developed Tool Room for developing new Tool & Die, along with its fixture and maintenance.

Our Machinery & Equipments

At Jyotika Appliances, we're stepping into a newer world with improved areas of manufacturing infused with state-of-the-art technology. This has aided us in delivering the best of offerings to our clients. The in-house tool room consists of Lathes, Drill Machines, Surface Grinders, tools, etc. enabling us to develop new Tools and Dies as and when required; the factory is equipped with the latest machines ranging from Power Presses, Channel Strip Beading Machines, Hydraulic Presses, Support Channels Machines, Spot Welding Machines, etc., thereby increasing the everyday production capacity of the plant.

Our Manpower

Consisting of both skilled and unskilled workers, the efficiency of the organization has grown from strength to strength with every passing day. The team at Jyotika Appliances consists of Production-In-charge, Supervisors, Pressmans, Spot welders, Die Makers, Tool Room Operators and Helpers.
